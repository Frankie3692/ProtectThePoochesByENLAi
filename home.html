Who are we

At "Protect the Pooches Institute", we are dedicated to providing love, care, and support to the most vulnerable members of our furry family. Every day, countless puppies find themselves in need of a helping hand, whether they're abandoned, injured, or simply in need of a loving home. Founded in 2012, this institute was founded to seek out kind souls, such as yourself, and put them towards a greater purpose. Through our tireless efforts and the generosity of our supporters, we strive to ensure that every puppy receives the care they deserve. From providing essential medical treatment to offering a warm bed and a wagging tail, we are committed to making a difference in the lives of these precious creatures. Join us in our mission to give every puppy the chance to thrive and find their forever home.

We Hope to Touch your Heart

Our mission is to be the unwavering voice and compassionate force for souls in need. They ran an algorithm and determined that a foundation for abused and homeless dogs would be the best way to attract the most amount of people for the institute. We are committed to providing a safe haven, essential care, and nurturing environments for every puppy that crosses our path. Through our dedication to rescue, rehabilitation, and rehoming, we strive to create a world where every puppy is valued, cherished, and given the opportunity to lead a fulfilling life. With unwavering resolve, we advocate for responsible pet ownership, promote education on animal welfare, and collaborate with communities to build a future where every puppy is loved, protected, and embraced as a cherished member of society.
